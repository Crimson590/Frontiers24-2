BlackJack!


You start with 2 random cards, each with a number 1 - 10.
You can draw more cards to increase your total number value.
However, you cannot exceed 21. If you do, you lose.
You can stop drawing cards at any time. The "Dealer" draws next.
They continue to draw until they either exceed 16 or they have a higher value than you.
If they exceed 16, they cannot draw any more, if they gain a higher number than you, they win
